Art Gallery App

 Welcome to our app Hello, welcome to our artist management app! Ready to explore the features that we have for you ? Instructions

1. Home Page:
Upon opening the app, you'll see the home page with greetings and instructions.
2. Navigation Bar:
At the bottom, there's a navigation bar with four buttons: - Home - Add Artist - Display Artist - Privacy and Security
3. Add Artist Page:
If you go to "Add Artist," you'll find a form to add artists. At the top, there's a bar with two buttons: - Add Artist - Update and Delete Artist
4. Display Artist Page:
Clicking "Display Artist" takes you to a page showing a table with all added artists. You'll also find a search page to look for any artist by name. At the top, there are buttons for "Display Artist" "Search" and "Display Featured Artist." The latter functions the same as "Display Artist" but only shows featured artists.
5. Privacy and Security Page:
By clicking "Privacy and Security," you'll find all information related to app privacy and security.

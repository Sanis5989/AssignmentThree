1-Adding an Artist:


Go to the "Add Artist" page.
Fill out the form with the required artist details, including name, musical genre, year of career start, etc.
Check the corresponding box to indicate whether the artist is "Regular" or "Featured".
Click the "Add Artist" button to save the information.

2-Updating or Deleting an Artist:

If you wish to modify or delete an existing artist, go to the "Update and Delete Artist" page.
Select the artist from the dropdown list.
If you're editing, update the necessary fields and then click "Confirm Update".
If you're deleting, simply click "Confirm Delete".


3-Viewing Artists:


On the "Display Artists" page, you have several options:
"Display All Artists": You will see a complete list of all added artists, whether regular or featured.
"Search Artist": Enter the exact name of the artist you wish to find.
"Display Featured Artists": Shows only the featured artists.
